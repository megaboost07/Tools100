About MEGA STORE

Your trusted platform for free online tools, built with passion and purpose

Our Mission

MEGA STORE was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to make professional-grade online tools accessible to everyone, completely free of charge. We believe that powerful software should not be locked behind paywalls or require complex installations. In today's digital world, everyone deserves access to quality tools that can help them work smarter and more efficiently.

Our platform provides over 50 carefully crafted tools spanning multiple categories including PDF manipulation, image processing, financial calculations, code generation, and security utilities. Each tool is designed with user experience in mind, ensuring that even complex operations can be performed with just a few clicks.

Our Story

MEGA STORE began as a small project in Silchar, Assam, with the goal of solving everyday digital challenges faced by students, professionals, and businesses across India. What started as a handful of utilities quickly grew into a comprehensive platform serving millions of users worldwide.

We are based in Assam, India, and our team is dedicated to continuously improving and expanding our tool collection based on user feedback and emerging needs. Our tools are used by students, teachers, business owners, developers, and creative professionals who need reliable, fast, and free solutions.

Why Choose Us

Unlike many other online tool platforms, MEGA STORE processes most operations directly in your browser. This means your files never leave your computer unless absolutely necessary, providing maximum security and privacy. When server processing is required, we use encrypted connections and immediately delete all uploaded files after processing.

Our platform is designed to be fast, reliable, and accessible. We optimize our code for performance, ensuring that even complex operations complete quickly. Whether you are merging PDFs, compressing images, or calculating EMI, you can expect instant results.
