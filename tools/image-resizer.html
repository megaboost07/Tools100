How to Use

  1. Upload your image using the upload area or drag and drop
  2. Enter your desired width and height in pixels
  3. Check 'Maintain aspect ratio' to keep proportions
  4. Click the 'Resize Image' button
  5. Download your resized image

Features

  • Resize to exact pixel dimensions
  • Maintain aspect ratio option
  • Supports JPG, PNG, WebP
  • Preview before downloading
  • Browser-based processing
  • 100% free

Benefits

Our Image Resizer is perfect for preparing images for websites, social media, email attachments, or any situation where you need specific dimensions. Keep your aspect ratio intact or set custom proportions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Enlarging images beyond their original size may reduce quality. Shrinking maintains quality. We use high-quality resizing algorithms for best results.

Currently, we process one image at a time for the best user experience. Simply repeat the process for additional images.

JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP, and WebP images are supported for resizing.
