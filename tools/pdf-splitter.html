PDF Splitter

Split PDF files into individual pages or extract ranges

Upload PDF File

Drag and drop a PDF file here or click to browse

How to Use

  1. Upload your PDF file using the upload area or drag and drop
  2. Choose your split mode: extract all pages individually or specify a page range
  3. Enter page range if needed (e.g., 1-3,5,7-9)
  4. Click the 'Split PDF' button
  5. Download your split PDF files

Features

  • Split PDFs into individual pages
  • Extract specific page ranges
  • All processing in browser - no upload
  • Download pages individually
  • Preserves original quality
  • 100% free

Benefits

Our PDF Splitter is perfect when you need to separate specific pages from a large document, extract chapters, or break a document into smaller parts for sharing.
