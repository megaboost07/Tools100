How to Use

  1. Select whether you want to add GST or remove GST from amount
  2. Enter the amount in rupees
  3. Select the applicable GST rate (5%, 12%, 18%, or 28%)
  4. Click 'Calculate GST'
  5. View the breakdown including CGST and SGST components

Features

  • Calculate GST inclusive and exclusive amounts
  • All GST rates supported (5%, 12%, 18%, 28%)
  • Automatic CGST and SGST split
  • Instant calculation
  • 100% free

Benefits

Essential for businesses, accountants, and shoppers in India. Quickly determine the exact GST component in any transaction, or compute the final price including GST.

Frequently Asked Questions

All major GST rates: 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28% as per Indian GST law.

GST is equally divided into CGST (Central) and SGST (State), each being half of the total GST rate.
