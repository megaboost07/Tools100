QR Code Generator

Generate QR codes for URLs, text, and more

How to Use

  1. Select the QR code type (URL, text, email, phone, or WiFi)
  2. Enter your content in the input field
  3. Adjust the size slider if needed
  4. Click 'Generate QR Code'
  5. Download your QR code as PNG

Features

  • Generate QR codes for URLs, text, email, phone, WiFi
  • Adjustable size from 128px to 1024px
  • High error correction level
  • Download as PNG image
  • 100% free

Benefits

QR codes are versatile tools for marketing, contact sharing, and quick access to digital content. Use them on business cards, flyers, posters, or websites to bridge the gap between physical and digital worlds.
