How to Use

  1. Enter the loan principal amount in rupees
  2. Enter the annual interest rate as a percentage
  3. Enter the loan tenure (in years or months)
  4. Click 'Calculate EMI'
  5. View your monthly EMI, total interest, and payment breakdown

Features

  • Calculate EMI for any loan amount
  • Support for both years and months tenure
  • Year-wise payment breakdown table
  • Total interest and payment calculation
  • Instant results
  • 100% free

Benefits

Planning to take a home loan, personal loan, or car loan? Our EMI Calculator helps you understand the exact monthly burden, total interest outgo, and plan your finances accordingly before committing to a loan.
